In order to properly use the extension and its functionality follow these steps.
-Click the Fb Login button in order to login with your facebook account and provide the neccessary permissions.
-Click the Save History button in order to fetch your history and save it to our database.
-Use the bar to adjust the percentage of your history that will be used in order to create your profile. In this way you can choose between a general or a more recent profile that best suits your needs.
-Click the Create Profile button in order create a personalized profile that corresponds with the user's domains of interest and expertise.
